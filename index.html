What is the credit spread calculator?
The credit spread calculator is a tool that computes the credit spread of a corporate bond before you make an investment decision.
How to calculate credit spread?
To calculate credit spread you should use the following formula:
credit spread = (corporate bond yield - government bond yield) * 100
Why use credit spread calculator?
The credit spread calculator is used in order to measure the credit spread of a corporate bond before you make an investment decision.
